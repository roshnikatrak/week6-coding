Rank-based test example (Mann-Whitney U)

set.seed(1414)
gp_1 <- sample.int(20, size=15, replace=TRUE)

set.seed(1424)
gp_2 <- sample.int(30, size=15, replace=TRUE)

hist(gp_2)

Reasons to use a rank-based test: 1. Not clearly normally distributed from exploratory histograms 2. Somewhat small sample size (n = 15 for each) 3. I’ve decided that ranks (or, medians) are a more valuable metric to compare for these data.

Mann-Whitney U Is there a significant difference in ranks (medians) between gp_1 and gp_2?
